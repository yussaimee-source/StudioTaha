About Assalamualaikum

Not merchandise.
Meaning, made tangible.

StudioTaha builds the identities that define how brands are seen. Assalamualaikum takes that work one step further — into the physical world, where design lives in the hands of real people, not just on screens.

The word Assalamualaikum is not just a name. It is the greeting that opens every meaningful encounter — a signal of care, respect, and presence. Every product we make carries that same spirit: a gesture that says something about who you are before you say a word.

How It Works

From idea to
your hands.

01

Brief

Tell us what you need — product type, quantity, intended use, and any design assets you already have. No brief is too small or too ambitious.

02

Design

Our team adapts or creates artwork specifically for the product format — considering print method, material, size, and how the design will hold up over time.

03

Sample

Before production begins, you approve a physical sample or digital proof. We only move forward when every detail is exactly right.

04

Deliver

Production, quality control, and delivery to your door. Every order is packed with the same care we put into designing it.

Our Values

Not just products.
Purposeful objects.

In a world drowning in cheap, disposable merch, Assalamualaikum takes the opposite position. The things a brand puts its name on should reflect the brand's own standard — no shortcuts, no compromise.

◈

Quality over quantity

We only work with materials and methods we'd put our own name on. Every product is sampled and approved before production.

◈

Design-first, always

Built on StudioTaha's creative rigour. Your product isn't just printed — it's designed, composed, and considered for the format.

◈

Consistency at every touchpoint

From the hang tag to the inner label to the outer box — every detail is a chance to reinforce who you are.

◈

Accessible for every scale

Solo founders to large organisations. No minimum order, no gatekeeping. Great physical branding isn't just for big budgets.
