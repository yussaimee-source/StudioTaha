The unboxing experience is your brand's first physical handshake with the customer. We design and produce packaging that makes that moment count — from rigid gift boxes and mailer boxes to tissue paper, sticker seals, and custom labels.

Every Salaam product begins with a design consultation — either using your existing brand assets or developed fresh by the StudioTaha creative team. We don't start production until you've approved a proof. Your brand deserves that care.

Production

All products are made to order. Production begins after artwork approval and payment confirmation. You'll receive a proof within 1 business day of placing your order.

Delivery

Peninsular Malaysia: 2–3 days via J&T / Poslaju. East Malaysia: 4–6 days. International shipping available on request. Tracking number provided upon dispatch.

Payment

Online banking via Maybank2U, CIMB Clicks, or RHB Now. DuitNow QR also accepted. Full payment required before production begins. Receipt issued on confirmation.

Returns & Revisions

Custom products are non-refundable once production begins. However, if we produce something that doesn't match the approved proof, we'll reprint at no cost. Your satisfaction is our guarantee.
